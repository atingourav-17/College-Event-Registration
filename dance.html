Hip Hop Battle

Immerse yourself in the raw energy of street dance. This high-octane event features the city's best b-boys and b-girls competing in a thrilling showdown of power moves, freezes, and footwork. Don't miss the open cyphers happening all evening!

Modern Expressions

A captivating evening dedicated to contemporary and modern expression. Witness groundbreaking choreography that challenges conventions and explores the depths of human emotion through fluid, abstract, and powerful movement. A must-see for lovers of avant-garde performance.

Classical Gala

Be transported by the timeless elegance and technical precision of classical dance. This gala performance features stunning excerpts from beloved ballets and showcases the intricate footwork and graceful lines of traditional forms. An enchanting experience of discipline and beauty.
