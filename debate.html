Arguments & Ideas

Explore different points of view.

Parliamentary Debate

Watch debaters think on their feet! In this style, teams get the topic only 15 minutes before they speak. It is a fast-paced and witty debate that is fun to watch, as speakers must come up with smart arguments very quickly.

Lincoln-Douglas Debate

This is a one-on-one debate that asks "what is right?" instead of "what will work?" Speakers argue about big ideas like justice, fairness, and freedom. It is a good debate for people who like to think about values and morals.

Policy Debate

See teams argue for and against a big change in government policy. This debate is all about facts and research. The teams spend a lot of time finding evidence to support their side. It is perfect for those who like to study a topic in great detail.
