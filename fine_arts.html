Art & Form

See the world's creativity.

Painting Showcase

Come see beautiful paintings from many talented artists. This event shows off amazing pictures made with oils, watercolors, and more. You will see bright colors and wonderful scenes that tell stories on canvas.

The Sculpture Garden

Walk through a garden filled with art you can touch! These 3D shapes are made from stone, wood, and metal. Each piece has a special form and looks different from every side. It's a fun way to see how artists build art.

Master Drawing Show

See the power of a simple pencil and paper. This show has detailed drawings of people, places, and ideas. You can see how artists use lines and shadows to make their pictures look real. It shows how much you can do with just a line.
